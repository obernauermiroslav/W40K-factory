Rules of the game:
It is simple, you have to beat the orcs, the task is to collect 1500+ soldiers faster that orcs can gather 1500+ grunts. You have to click the button:"create soldiers", each click will
create 10 soldiers, if you get 50, you can hire an officer(click the button:"hire officer"), he will provide you with more soldiers(increases the speed by 10/s).
You can also cheat, by clicking the button:"For the emperor"(it will be unlocked after reaching certain number of soldiers, and it will dramatically increase the speed).
